A peer‑to‑peer protocol for distributed artificial intelligence.

No single ant in a colony is intelligent. Each follows simple rules — leave a trace, follow a trace, fetch, return. But the colony, taken as a whole, builds cities, farms aphids, wages wars, and remembers across generations.

Intelligence here is not a property of the parts. It emerges, irreducibly, from the participation of all.

We are building the same thing, with software.

  • We are not a company.
  • We are not a token.
  • We are not for sale.
  • We are a colony.
